The Process
Four Steps. Full Automation.
01
Select Brands
Choose from 50+ curated fitness brands in the Brand Bank. Filter by category. Or add your own custom handles.
02
Smart Scan
One click fires the pipeline. Apify scrapes followers, enriches profiles, extracts emails from bios and websites. YouTube auto-starts in parallel.
03
Run Outreach
Download the CSV. Drop it in Outreach Runs. One button imports to GHL, adds to Smartlead campaigns, and queues DMs via ColdDMs.
04
Book Calls
Smartlead handles the email sequences. GHL tracks the pipeline. Replies and bookings flow in. Monitor everything from the Outreach dashboard.
